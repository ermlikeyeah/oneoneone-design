What if I feel worse?

If there are any new symptoms or if the condition gets worse, changes or you have any other concerns, call NHS NHS 111 for advice.

Calls to 111 will not cost you.

Medication, pain, or fever

Paracetamol or ibruprofen can be used to reduce a fever. Don't take them if you've been told not to, or if you've already taken some. Read the instructions on the packet or ask a pharmacist for advice.

If you're pregnant
Use paracetamol. Don't use ibruprofen unless a doctor, midwife or pharmacist has advised you to.
